Privacy Policy

Effective June 23, 2026 · Last updated July 20, 2026

Vita Nova is a fertility charting tool. We know that cycle data — observations, peak days, intercourse — is among the most personal health information you have. This policy explains exactly how your data is stored, who can see it, and what we do (and don't do) with it.

Short version: your chart lives on your device. If you sign in for cloud backup, the data is encrypted on your device before it is uploaded — we cannot read it, and neither can anyone who accesses the cloud database.

1. No account required

By default, Vita Nova stores your chart exclusively in your browser's localStorage. No account is created, no data leaves your device, and no server ever sees your observations. Clearing your browser data will erase your chart — so use the export feature if you want a backup.

2. Optional cloud backup (sign-in)

You may choose to sign in with Google or an email and password to back up your chart to the cloud and access it on multiple devices. When you do:

  • Your data is encrypted on your device before it is uploaded, using AES-256-GCM (the same standard used by banks and governments). The encryption key is generated on your device and stored only in your browser — it never leaves your device.
  • Our cloud database (Google Firebase Firestore) stores an encrypted blob. Even Google, Firebase, or we cannot read the contents of your chart without your device's key.
  • Your email address and account information are stored by Firebase Authentication in order to identify your account. This is standard for any sign-in system.

3. What data we collect

  • If you never sign in: nothing. All data stays in your browser.
  • If you sign in: your email address (or Google account identifier), and an encrypted copy of your chart. The chart contains whatever you have entered: flow type, mucus observations, peak day markers, intercourse indicators, and any free-text notes.

We do not use analytics, crash reporting, advertising trackers, or any other third-party scripts beyond Firebase (authentication and encrypted storage).

4. Who can see your data

  • You. Only someone with your signed-in device (which holds the encryption key) can decrypt your chart.
  • Google / Firebase stores the encrypted blob as infrastructure. They cannot read the contents. Their privacy policy governs their handling of infrastructure data: policies.google.com/privacy.
  • No one else. We do not sell, share, rent, or otherwise disclose your data to any third party for any purpose.

5. Data retention and deletion

  • To delete your local chart: use the "Reset all data" option in the app.
  • To delete your cloud account and all stored data: sign in, then use the delete account option in your profile, or email us and we will remove it within 30 days.
  • Clearing your browser's site data removes the local chart and the encryption key. If the key is gone, the cloud copy cannot be decrypted — it becomes permanently inaccessible.

6. Children

Vita Nova is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has created an account, contact us and we will delete it.

7. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after a change constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Contact

Questions or deletion requests: support@thomistico.com
